question: "Can I use IPTV Espana on multiple devices?"
category: "technical"
order: 6

Yes, IPTV Espana allows you to use the service on multiple devices, depending on the subscription plan you choose. This multi-device capability means you can enjoy your favorite content on different devices within the same household or on the go. Each plan may have specific multi-device usage restrictions, so it is advisable to check the terms relevant to your selected package.
